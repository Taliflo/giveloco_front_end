How It Works

1. Purchase

Browse businesses to see which causes they sponsor and how much of each purchase will go to the cause. When you purchase a gift certificate from that business, you choose the cause and your money goes directly to the them.

2. Redeem

To redeem, simply show your gift certificate that we emailed to you to the cashier. If you opted to use Taliflo's web, iOS or Android apps, simply enter the amount of the purchase and show the cashier the auto-generated serial number.
